Please see below for information on PNG UChicago's Fall 2023  Recruitment Cycle! 

Applications are now open and can be viewed in the links at the bottom of this email!!!


Paragon National Group is an intercollegiate student-run multi-strategy investment organization with $30,000 in AUM, currently with chapters at the University of Chicago, University of Pennsylvania, New York University, and Columbia University. PNG focuses on both fundamental investing and systematic algorithmic trading strategies and invests out of two funds: the PNG Value Fund—a long-only fundamental investing fund—and the PNG Systematic Fund—a fund that deploys low and mid-frequency systematic trading algorithms.

PNG is currently sponsored by firms such as Citadel, Jane Street, DRW, Kershner Trading, CloudQuant, Kirkland and Ellis, Visible Alpha, Elevate, and WSO. Our members have gone on to place at firms like Goldman Sachs, Morgan Stanley, JP Morgan, Blackstone, Carlyle, Citadel, Jane Street, DE Shaw, SIG, Virtu, Meta, Microsoft, and many other top firms across finance, quant, and technology!

PNG is recruiting two cohorts this upcoming fall, the education application and the direct fund application. The education program is a 6-week educational program that covers fundamental investing as well as applied quantitative trading. The direct-fund application allows for students to apply to directly join the investment funds, where they can either do fundamental research or research systematic trading algorithms into either of our funds. We expect no experience for the education application but do expect some experience of financial markets for the fund application.

As a note, PNG collects Bi-Annual resumes books for our sponsors Citadel, Jane Street, and DRW, so please stay subscribed to this email list to be notified when we do!
