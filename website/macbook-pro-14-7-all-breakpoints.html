About Us

Paragon as an intercollegiate student-run investment club with chapters at the University of Chicago and the University of Pennsylvania that utilizes both fundamental and quantitative-based research strategies to invest into our 20,000 dollar investment fund.

Sponsors

Education

Value Investment

Learn about the basic of accounting, valuation, modeling, and bottom-up analysis of companies. Students will learn to develop value-based investment research on publicly traded companies

Software Development

Learn how to build applications and conduct complex statistical analysis using Python. Students will learn to extract unique insight from financial data.

Quantitative Finance

Learn the basic of modern portfolio theory, linear factor pricing models, and factor portfolio construction algorithms. Students will be able to build software applications that systematically construct a portfolio of securities.

Investment Strategy
Factor Portfolio Construction

A factor portfolio construction algorithm will analyze all public companies identify a universe (”factor basket”) of 100-200 equities that have potential quantitative upside.

Fundamental Analysis

Students will utilize a bottom-up fundamental approach to conduct competitor analysis, build relative and intrinsic valuation models, and identify mispriced assets.

Portfolio Allocation

Once a select number of assets are selected, a novel portfolio allocation algorithm, based off mean-variance, determines individual asset weightings to optimize out-of-sample total portfolio Sharpe.
