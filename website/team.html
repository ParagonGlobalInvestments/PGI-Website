Leadership

Jay Sivadas

Co-President

Jay Sivadas is a sophomore studying Economics and Computer Science. Apart from serving as the Co-President of PNG, he is a member of The Blue Chips Investment Club and the Quantitative Trading focused Financial Markets Program. He currently works at Golub Capital, a 45bn+ AUM credit asset manager. He has also worked as a research assistant for the UChicago Computer Science Department, a long-short investment analyst, and as a data science intern. At Paragon, he coleads the computer and data science development program.

Daniel Labrador

Co-President

Daniel Labrador is a third year Economics student in the college. On campus he is involved in The Alpha Kappas Psi Business Fraternity, The Blue Chips Investing Club, as well as the Sigma Chi Fraternity. In addition to his extracurriculars he was also selected into the prestigious Chicago Booth Dougan Scholars Program. Next summer Daniel will be working as an Investment Banking Summer Analyst at the Bank of America in their New York Office and he hopes to pursue a full time career in Investment Banking.

Erin Ku

Co-President

Erin Ku is a second-year at the College majoring in Business Economics and Data Science. Apart from serving as the Co-President of PNG, she is also involved in the Executive Board as the Social VP of the business fraternity Delta Sigma Pi. She is also the member of the Trott Business Program, the Delta Gamma sorority, Scholars of Finance, and Women in Business. This upcoming summer, Erin will be a Summer Intensive Program Scholar at Girls Who Invest as she pursues her career in Investment Banking.
