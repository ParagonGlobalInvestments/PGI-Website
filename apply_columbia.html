Paragon Columbia Winter/Spring 2024 Recruiting Cycle

Education Application

The education application is for admittance to the education portion of Paragon. The education is a 6-week curriculum that covers both fundamental investing and quantative finance.

Student's who succesfully complete the education portion will have the opportunity to join their preferred investment fund. For more information, see the education application below.

NO Experience is required!

Direct Fund Application

The fund application is for admittance directly into the investment research groups for Paragon where you will dive right in to researching companies or algorithms that will be invested into or deployed out of the investment funds.

Experience is required!

You can choose to directly apply to the Paragon Value Fund or the PNG Systematic Fund. More information is available on the application form.
